# EU DECIDO

Experimental digital democracy platform powered by open technologies and responsible AI.

---

## Vision

EU DECIDO is an experimental civic technology initiative exploring new forms of democratic participation through open technologies, transparent governance mechanisms, and responsible artificial intelligence.

The project aims to create digital infrastructures that allow citizens to:
- participate continuously in democratic processes,
- propose and debate ideas,
- vote transparently and securely,
- and collectively shape governance structures.

EU DECIDO combines:
- digital participation,
- anonymous voting systems,
- community deliberation,
- and future multi-agent AI architectures aligned with European values and Responsible AI principles.

---

# Core Principles

- Citizen participation beyond election cycles
- Transparency and auditability
- Responsible and explainable AI
- European technological sovereignty
- Privacy and GDPR compliance
- Open collaboration and civic co-creation
- Human-centered decision making

# Current Architecture

## Mobile Application
- Kotlin Multiplatform
- Compose Multiplatform
- Offline-first architecture

## Backend
- FastAPI
- PostgreSQL
- Supabase
- JWT authentication
- Anonymous voting mechanisms

## AI Infrastructure (In Development)
- Multi-agent orchestration
- Compatibility with European open-source models:
  - Mistral
  - EuroLLM
  - Llama
  - Ollama-based deployments
